  • Introduction

    The crime reporting application is a simple software that helps the public in the reporting of criminal activities. The general objective is to allow the public to report activates anonymously easily and in real-time.

  • How It Works

    When an individual reports a criminal activity, the record is stored, and another person can view when a need arises. Once a reported crime activity is deleted, the application updates the records. The users can also view the reported crime activities, post, and comment on the reported criminal activities. Also, the users can chat with each other concerning the criminal activities reported and other relevant information.
